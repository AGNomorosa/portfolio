From Discovery to Design

Ritmo DMV is a Latin dance company based in the Washington, DC metro area. Judicita Condezo, the founder and Artistic Director, has taken her company to perform around the country and around the world. Ritmo DMV requested a website that would be capable of showcasing their photos and videos of their performances while having a responsive framework to allow their current and prospective clients to access their site on multiple devices and media. Ritmo DMV requested a Wordpress framework because of their familiarity and preference to a CMS.

Development And
Wordpress Integration

Ritmo DMV also wanted their site to be a hub of information, a source for their user base to visit after following and connecting with them through social media. The goal was to not only connect and grow their fan base, but to be professional and reach out to their clients, both current and potential, and book potential shows.
