From Discovery to Design

Twitch Gaming Experience is a small business located in Edinburgh, UK. Ewan McMahon and Alan McLevy offer Internet and printing services, but they are best known as the interactive gaming hub of Edinburgh. They requested a compile re-design of their website from the ground up, but manage to remain consistent with their branding and identity. The primary objective was to incorporate a responsive framework within a Wordpress CMS, because a large number of their clientele were young adults, who often used tablets and mobile devices to view their site.

Twitch's audience did request one feature, which was to include a comprehensive catalog of available programs and video games that were available on all of the platforms that they offered. The goal was to present their gaming catalog, with their promotions, and customer rates in a comprehensive, clear manner.
