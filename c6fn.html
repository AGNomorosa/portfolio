C6 Fight Night

C6 Fight Night is a gaming and adventure community based in the Washington, DC metro area. Their activities range from LAN tournaments to camping excursions to board game nights. The primary goal was to present their identity by capturing their enthusiasm and intensity within their organization as well as their forward-thinking attitude.
