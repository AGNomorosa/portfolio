Creative Process

Explore.

Inspiration can be found anywhere. Different areas of the world apply amazing design solutions to all types of media, from mobile UI to video game HUDs. I live for the adventure of adopting and discovering new design approaches while applying them to new technologies.

Learn.

Research, research, research. Defining distinct goals and objectives allow the best approach to be taken when designing and building a website. With new design applications and frameworks continually emerging, there is never a shortage from which to learn and experiment.

Build.

Designing and developing a functional website requires me to have a thorough Discovery meetup with clients before beginning a new project. The majority of a website's design and architecture is conceptual, where every design conundrum is a problem to be solved.

Grow.

Rarely does everything go according to plan. Therefore, the creative post-mortem process is essential to improving the fluidity of the project process and to better client experiences with efficient, functional solutions.

My Story

My name is Anthony Nomorosa and I am a freelance designer and front-end developer based in the San Francisco Bay area. You will often find me listening to indie and hip hop music while watching eSports and jumping on opportunities to explore the world. I thrive on learning and experiencing functional design and emerging technologies that enable me to build websites for a diverse client base. I pour my heart and soul into my work by striving to build clean, functional UI for a variety of platforms and devices. I have experience with the Foundation 4 framework, SASS and implementing Wordpress websites. I am more than happy to utilize my technical skills and artistic abilities to help in the design or redesign of your website or branding and identity.
