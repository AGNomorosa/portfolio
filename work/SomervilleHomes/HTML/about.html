At Somerville Homes, our goal is to provide you with personal service and attention to detail to make the building process an enjoyable experience. Owner, Dan Somerville, will walk you through the process and be your main point of contact from start to finish. Dan brings over 15 years of construction experience in both commercial and residential building. He graduated from the Virginia Tech College of Architecture and Urban Studies, Department of Building Construction, and spent time as a commercial project manager prior to building custom homes across the Northern Virginia area. Dan understands that your dream home is as unique and individual as you are and is committed to helping you turn that dream into reality.
