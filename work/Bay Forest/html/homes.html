The best selling new beach home community in the area.

Home is where the heart is and you'll fall in love with the homes at Bay Forest. From a charming bungalow to a luxurious cape, the home you want is here, with architectural details and amenities that fit right in at the beach. Single Family Homes, Villa Homes, Cottage Homes and Townhomes have been designed into a series of discrete, pedestrian friendly neighborhoods, separated by attractive environmental features and open space. The best Delaware beach homes are for sale at Bay Forest priced from the $200s.

  • Located along the Southern edge of the Indian River Bay.
  • 400 acres of forested woodlands and open meadows.
  • Features an extensive path system.
  • Pedestrian bridges provide access to Collins Creek.
