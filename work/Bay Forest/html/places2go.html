Main Street Homes

Shop 'til you drop, play a round, enjoy the local cuisine and party all night. There truly is
something for everyone in Bethany Beach and the Atlantic beach coastline.

From quaint shops to outlet malls, the choices are plentiful.

Fast food, casual and high end restaurants and cafes in Bethany Beach, Fenwick Island and Ocean City abound.

Fun for the whole family includes amusement parks, boardwalks and speedways.

The area comes alive at night with myriad theaters and clubs.

Take pleasure in several scenic state parks just minutes away.

From public courses to country clubs, we've got challenging courses, no matter what your handicap.

Come and experience the area's rich history and beauty by visiting the many museums and historical sites.

Enjoy a variety of Year-round events at the Beach.

The area is considered a prime destination for resort homes or retirement due to its economic advantages.
