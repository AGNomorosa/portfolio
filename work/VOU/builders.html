Builders
Architectural guidelines mandate traditional design standards.

Thanks to the area's finest homebuilders, new home designs are crafted to make the most of the unique, natural setting, and to blend with the original "village" of the town of Urbana.

Townhome condominiums are offered for those desiring very low maintenance. A charming array of townhome and single family home designs are offered for those desiring smaller lots. Larger, more traditional style townhome and single family home designs are offered for those desiring larger lots.
