Desiging the Political Climate

NextGen Climate is a non-partisan organization focused on bringing climate change to the forefront of American politics and acts politically to avert climate disaster and preserve American prosperity. As the Interactive Designer, I'm responsible for creating and maintaining the visual messaging across Facebook, Twitter, and Wordpress platforms. I also provided graphics for national and state press releases. Our goal was to inspire grassroots voting movements and highlight political candidates on their stances on climate change.
