Californians Demand a Fair Shake

Fair Shake is an advocacy campaign for California taxpayers to claim over $228,000 in revenue every hour because global oil companies aren't accountable in paying an extraction tax when extracting California's natural resources. The campaign required identity development and a corresponding landing page. The goal of the campaign was to spread awareness and provide a platform for California citizens to participate and vote to institue an extraction tax for global oil companies.
